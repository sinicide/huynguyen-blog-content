## Intro
This year has been absolutely a joke for America and not in a good way. So while this country continues to crash out, there's been some good things I've enjoyed this year. Let's get started!

## Favorite Movies I watched

### Weapons (2025)

One of my favorite horror movies of this year, not specifically because it was scary, but it played with some interesting ideas and that ending sequence, so good.

{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/sinners.jpg" alt="Sinners (2025)" >}}

### Beginners (2010)
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/beginners.jpg" alt="Beginners (2010)" >}}
I re-watched this movie this year and I'm still enamored about this movie. It's such a good story about allowing one's self to open up to the opportunity of something. Ewan McGregor who plays the main character, is depicted as this kinda depressed, quirky, plain human and how he tells the story of the events unfolding is lovely. The subplot about the late stage life of his father is just fantastic. If you're a quirky kinda romantic, I think this is a good one to watch.

### Babyteeth (2020)

I don't want to give much away about it, but it deals with a young girl's late stage cancer and how she is dealing with it. As much as it was about Eliza Scanlen's character, Milla, the story told of the parents dealing with the inevitable loss of their child was so great. The ending scenes in this movie really got me.

### The Friend (2024)
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/the_friend.jpg" alt="The Friend (2024)" >}}

The main challenge was that Naomi's character, Iris has to deal with wishes of her dear departed friend and take care of this old Great Dane. Having lost my dog in recent years, it really got to me.

## Favorite Shows I watched
I watched a lot of shows this year, so I've tried to keep this list tidy. Here we go!

### The Residence
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/thhe_residence.jpg" alt="The Residence" >}}
Staring Uzo Aduba as Cordelia Cupp, this detective murder mystery series was amazing. I loved everything about it, the quirkiness of the characters, the mystery of the murder, it was just great. I really wished people enjoyed it more to get a second season.

### Alien: Earth
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/alien_earth.jpg" alt="Alien: Earth" >}}
First ever Alien franchise television series, yes please. While there was a lot to accept about this story, android children and taking place on Earth being some of the least desirable plot points to me, what they did really well was the inclusion of so many weird extraterrestrial species and I mean eyeball alien is definitely my new favorite.

### The Studio
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/the_studio.jpg" alt="The Studio" >}}
Seth Rogen's comedy satire about hollywood was an amazing rollercoaster ride of a show, each episode was absolutely insane.

### Normal People (2020)
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/normal_people.jpg" alt="Normal People (2020)" >}}
I've heard high praises about this romance series, so I finally got around to checking it. I have to say that I really enjoyed the long drawn romance taking place over the course of high school to uni to after. I liked the complexity of the 2 main characters, Connell and Marianne, they're also very Neurodivergent coded.

### Star Trek: Discovery
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/star_trek_discovery.jpg" alt="Star Trek: Discovery" >}}
So I got on a Star Trek kick this year and watched all of Discovery and then later Strange New Worlds. I really really enjoyed the characters and world building they did in Discovery. What's great about Star Trek as a "space" franchise is that it's always more so focused on hope and the possibility of adventure as opposed to Star Wars being more focused on battle and attrition. You can't help but watch an episode and be wowed.

### Star Trek: Strange New Worlds
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/star_trek_strange_new_worlds.jpg" alt="Star Trek: Strange New Worlds" >}}
For as great as Discovery is, I think Strange New Worlds is my particular jam. Seeing a young Spock nail the weirdness/struggle of his character is amazing. As always each character on the series is just fantastic, top notch.

### King of the Hill (Old + Revival)
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/king_of_the_hill.jpg" alt="King of the Hill" >}}
When I heard that King of the Hill was getting a revival and picking up several years after the series ended. I had to go back and re-watch all of it all. The old series still holds up. The new season was amazing, they really aged the characters in terms of progression and character development really well. The voice actor shuffling wasn't bad, I understand why the did and don't have any issues there. I really enjoyed how Bobby went from this kinda unsure of himself and sometimes cocky kid to this stressed out adult trying to build something he can be proud of and living life. I can't wait to see what they do for another new season and who they can bring back.

## Favorite Things I read
This year I read a few different mediums, but only a handful of them I would call my favorite, so I'm combining comics, manga and novels this year.

## Favorite Anime I watched

### Tojima Wants to Be a Kamen Rider
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/tojima.jpg" alt="Tojima Wants to Be a Kamen Rider" >}}
This is hands down my favorite Anime of this year. It's extremely meta crafting a story in a fictional world that grew up with the Kamen Rider franchise, so you have these fanatic who's main thing is that they aspire to be like their idol Kamen Riders. All while finding out that their world actually has the alien enemies from the show. So now these adults who worshiped Kamen Rider can now live out their power fantasy of becoming a Kamen Rider and defeating evil. The music and power ups and fights goes so hard in this Anime. Also the fact that the main character is in his thirties is also a plus, it's kinda refreshing to watch anime of age appropriate people to yourself.

### Yowamushi Pedal
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/yowamushi_pedal.jpg" alt="Yowamushi Pedal" >}}
I re-watch this anime from time to time and this year, its just one of those wholesome sports anime that makes you want to get out there and do something.

### My Hero Academia (Series Finale)
{{< image src="https://r2.huynguyen.me/blog/2025/12/24/favorite-thangs-in-2025/images/my_hero.jpg" alt="My Hero Academia (Series Finale)" >}}
The manga finished publication last year so the anime was finally wrapping up this year. Seeing the action scenes animated from the manga was something amazing. My Hero gets kinda a bad wrap because of the fanbase or because it's a typical Shonen, but it hits all the notes for me.
